01. Project Overview
Background
Wells Fargo is an American, multinational financial services company that provides banking, mortgage, investing, credit card, and personal, small business, and commercial financial services. The Wells Fargo mobile app allows clients to manage finances, make deposits, transfer funds, and pay bills. Within this past year, Wells Fargo has taken the first steps to rebranding themselves after recovering from their infamous bank scandal. Theyâ€™ve slightly updated their logo, featuring a more bold and modern red hue as well as a new graphic design treatment for their wagon visual.
Objective
Despite this update in brand identity, their mobile app has yet to be modernized. The current design is quite dated in terms of interface and usability, making it complicated for users to navigate through and quickly find what they need. The purpose of this redesign is to simplify and streamline navigation of the app, focus its layout around the most interaction-heavy features, and update the interface to increase user engagement.
