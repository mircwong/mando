Welcome

Nice to meet you
I’m Miranda, a recent graduate from UC Davis where I majored in Design and Business Economics. I’m currently a product designer at OpenMeal, a non-profit organization leveraging technology to serve meals to those in need while also supporting local, family-owned restaurants!
In the fall, I'll be joining the UX Design team at Workday as an Associate Product Designer! I'm so grateful for this opportunity and I'm excited to learn and grow in this new role.
For me, design is about finding and doing meaningful work. My goal, be it in design or otherwise, is to improve public life for everyday people and that's evident in the work I've done thus far. And what's even more energizing is being able to work on projects I truly care about and understanding experiences I might have never related to otherwise.
