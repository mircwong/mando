Pleased to meet you!😄
I'm Miranda, a senior at the University of California, Davis double majoring in Design and Managerial Economics. I'm an experience and interaction designer interested in designing for public good and to make a greater impact. I am currently VP Education + a Design Consultant at Design Interactive, UC Davis’ first student-run human-centered design consultancy. Our teams tackle client-based design projects every quarter and run studios and workshops for students interested in user experience. In June, my team and I completed our redesign of the mobile app at JAPA, a smart parking service.

I am also a Design Intern at Venture Catalyst, a branch of the UC Davis Office of Research that facilitates the translation of university research and technology by driving the development of new ventures. I work with researchers and innovators launching new startups in the biopharma and biotech industry through UC Davis to design strategic branding, which include logos, signage, pitchdecks, etc.

My journey began in high school, where I designed custom t-shirts for my friends, sports teams, and student organizations on campus. ‍ Even though it was just a hobby, it's what got me curious about what design could do for people. I've learned that what I love about design is that it's extremely versatile and ubiquitous and I think there's a lot of power in that; power that should be used for good. 💖

I seek a deep understanding of the world, both the good and the bad, and hope to be able to make it better. As I learn more about people, society, and systems, I desire sincerity and honesty in all facets of my life and hope to apply those qualities to my work.
