I'm Miranda, a junior at the University of California, Davis double majoring in Design and Managerial Economics. I'm a UX and visual designer interested in designing for public good and to make a greater impact at any scale. I am a curious and analytical designer who is able to synthesize different perspectives and design for other people in mind. I am committed to putting thoughtful design first, every time, and the interdisciplinary quality of human-centered design allows me to adapt to the language of designers, researchers, makers, and developers.

My design experience at UC Davis Venture Catalyst, a branch of the UC Davis Office of Research that facilitates the translation of university research and technology by driving the development of new ventures, has enabled me to push toward my end goal of leaving a greater impact, using design to help people, and working collaboratively to empower people.

I seek a deep understanding of the world, both the good and the bad, and hope to be able to make it better. As I learn more about people, society, and systems, I desire sincerity and honesty in all facets of my life and hope to apply those qualities to my work.
